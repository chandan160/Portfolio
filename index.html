01 About

Bridging AI &
Clinical Reality

I am an AI Researcher with over 1.5 years of hands-on research experience at BBAU, Lucknow,funded by Department of Science and Technology, Delhi, focused on using deep learning to solve real-world medical challenges — particularly in neuromuscular disorder diagnosis and computational genetics.

My work spans Generative AI, Computer Vision, NLP, and Medical Imaging. I design and train architectures based on artificial intelligence for the diagnosis of Medical disease specifically Neuromuscular Disorders.

Beyond model accuracy, I care deeply about explainability — ensuring AI tools can be trusted in clinical workflows. I have contributed to multiple peer-reviewed publications and continue to push the boundaries of AI-assisted diagnosis.

NMDNet

End-to-end CNN model for automated neuromuscular disorder classification from ultrasound images. Achieved 94% accuracy and 0.97 ROC-AUC. Integrated LIME for clinical explainability and transparency.

CNN LIME PyTorch Medical Imaging

VAE-based FSHD Diagnosis

Applied Variational Autoencoders to overcome the curse of dimensionality in microarray gene expression datasets. Trained downstream classifiers on learned latent representations, achieving high accuracy in FSHD early diagnosis.

VAE Microarray Scikit-learn TensorFlow
GitHub
Research Communicated

MicroNMDNet

WT-GAN based data augmentation pipeline for FSHD gene expression classification. Combines Wavelet Transformation with a Generative Adversarial Network to synthesize balanced samples, significantly improving diagnostic accuracy.

GAN Wavelet Transform Gene Expression PyTorch
GitHub

YouNotes

AI-powered YouTube video summarizer web app. Extracts captions via PyTube, then leverages the BART large language model to produce concise, structured summaries — turning hour-long lectures into digestible notes.

BART PyTube NLP Flask

Image Caption Generator

Deep learning image captioning system using ResNet101 as an image encoder and a Transformer decoder. Trained on MS-COCO dataset for descriptive caption generation. Deployed with an interactive Streamlit UI.
